Ejercicios de Tiempos Verbales en Inglés

Completa las siguientes secciones para practicar Presente Simple, Presente Continuo, Presente Perfecto, Pasado Simple y Pasado Continuo.

1. Rellenar huecos (Verbos regulares, irregulares y adverbios)

Escribe la forma correcta del verbo entre paréntesis en el tiempo verbal adecuado.

  1. The doctor usually (arrive) at the hospital at 8:00 AM.
  2. Right now, the neurologist (examine) a patient carefully.
  3. I (already / finish) that new video game.
  4. Yesterday, we (play) online for three hours.
  5. While she (study), the phone suddenly rang.
  6. He (go) to the store yesterday because he needed milk.
  7. They (never / see) such a beautiful landscape before.
  8. Look! It (rain) heavily outside.
  9. I (walk) quickly down the street when I saw the accident.
  10. She (always / wake up) early on weekends.

2. Estructurar frases

Ordena las palabras para formar oraciones correctas en el tiempo indicado.

  1. (Past Simple) yesterday / they / football / played / happily
  2. (Present Perfect) yet / not / have / eaten / we / our dinner
  3. (Present Continuous) the / listening / is / carefully / doctor
  4. (Simple Present) usually / morning / reads / he / the newspaper / in the
  5. (Past Continuous) TV / watching / were / when / arrived / you / I

3. Traducción y opciones

Selecciona la opción correcta.

1. "Ella estaba trabajando cuando llegué."

She works when I arrive.
She was working when I arrived.
She has worked when I was arriving.

2. "Él nunca ha jugado ese juego."

He never played that game.
He doesn't ever play that game.
He has never played that game.

3. "They constantly complain about the weather."

Ellos se quejaron constantemente del clima.
Ellos se quejan constantemente del clima.
Ellos se estaban quejando del clima.

4. "I had a coffee yesterday morning."

Tomé un café ayer por la mañana.
He tomado un café ayer por la mañana.
Estaba tomando un café mañana.

4. Describe la imagen

Observa la descripción de la imagen y responde usando Presente Continuo.

[Imagen: Un hombre corriendo rápidamente bajo la lluvia]

What is the man doing? (run / quickly / in the rain)

[Imagen: Dos personas jugando a videojuegos con mandos]

What are they doing right now? (play / video games)
