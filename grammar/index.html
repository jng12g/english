Gramática

Los tiempos verbales
del inglés

Todo el sistema en una cuadrícula: 3 tiempos (presente, pasado, futuro) por 4 aspectos (simple, continuo, perfecto y perfecto continuo).

Simple Continuo Perfecto Perfecto continuo
Presente
→ Present Simple Presente simple I work → Present Continuous Presente continuo I am working → Present Perfect Pretérito perfecto I have worked → Present Perfect Cont. Pret. perfecto continuo I have been working
Pasado
→ Past Simple Pasado simple I worked → Past Continuous Pasado continuo I was working → Past Perfect Pluscuamperfecto I had worked → Past Perfect Cont. Pluscuamperfecto continuo I had been working
Futuro
→ Future Simple Futuro simple I will work → Future Continuous Futuro continuo I will be working → Future Perfect Futuro perfecto I will have worked → Future Perfect Cont. Futuro perfecto continuo I will have been working
